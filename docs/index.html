About Gotta

Gotta is a tiered task management app designed to help you organize and prioritize your life. Instead of one overwhelming list, organize your tasks into three priority levels: Daily (high priority), Weekly (medium priority), and Monthly (low priority).

Break down your responsibilities into manageable chunks, so you can focus on what needs attention now while keeping an eye on the bigger picture.

Key Features

  • Tiered Task Organization - Daily, Weekly, and Monthly priority levels
  • Smart Task Visibility - Tasks automatically appear in your daily view when deadlines approach
  • Category Organization - Color-coded categories to organize tasks
  • Intelligent Reminders - Get notified about deadlines and recurring tasks
  • Beautiful Design - Clean, modern interface built with SwiftUI
  • Task Carry-Forward - Incomplete tasks automatically carry forward
  • Deadline Tracking - Never miss a deadline with smart reminders

Perfect For

  • Professionals managing work and personal tasks
  • Students organizing assignments and deadlines
  • Anyone looking to improve productivity
  • People who want a simple, focused task manager
